## Sector Analysis
The industry and manufacturing sector sells fabrication capacity, quality, and reliability. The website must showcase facilities, capabilities, and certifications. Target audience: buyers, suppliers, candidates, investors.

## Template Objectives
- Show production capabilities and equipment
- Communicate quality, certifications, and standards
- Facilitate contact for quotes and partnerships
- Attract talent and suppliers

## Recommended Pages
1. **Homepage** — Industrial hero + capabilities + key figures + certifications + CTA quote
2. **Capabilities** — Production, machining, assembly, quality control, R&D
3. **Sectors Served** — Aerospace, automotive, energy, medical, agri-food, etc.
4. **Quality & Certifications** — ISO, standards, quality process, audits
5. **Equipment** — Machine park, technologies, automation, digital
6. **Sustainability** — CSR, environment, circular economy
7. **Careers** — Jobs, recruitment, training, safety
8. **About** — History, values, team, figures, governance
9. **Contact** — Quote form, contact details, map

## Priority Features
- Capability catalog with photos and videos of equipment
- Certification and standards grid
- Key figures (surface area, machines, employees, production)
- Industrial client testimonials
- Quote form with technical specifications
- Photo and video gallery of the factory
- Grid of served sectors with references
- Location map

## User Profiles
- **Buyer/Contract giver**: evaluates capabilities, requests quote
- **Supplier**: looks for partnership opportunities
- **Candidate**: looking for a job in industry
- **Investor**: evaluating the company for investment

## Design Direction
- **Palette**: Industrial gray (#374151) + Safety orange (#F97316) + Tech blue (#0284C7) + White
- **Typography**: Technical and robust sans-serif, solid titles
- **Mood**: Industrial, robust, precise, innovative, reliable
- **Images**: Modern factory, machines in action, team in PPE, finished products

## UX/UI Inspirations
- Safran-group.com — Aero and high technology
- Michelin.com — Industry and quality
- Schneider-electric.com — Industrial innovation
- Siemens.com — Diversification and technology

## Reference Market Sites
- **Valeo (valeo.com)**: Architecture by product line and automotive segment, innovation highlighted, Industry 4.0, careful engineer recruitment
- **Schneider Electric (se.com)**: Exhaustive technical catalog with configurator, rich technical documentation, client portal, exemplary multilingual, well-integrated CSR
- **Fives Group (fivesgroup.com)**: Well differentiated industrial sectors, solid client references, highlighted innovation
- **Lisi Group (lisi-group.com)**: Well displayed certifications, clearly presented aero/medical/auto sectors, serious CSR

## Typography Recommendation
Sans-serif engineering fonts (IBM Plex, DM Sans, Barlow) — readability of technical tables

## SEO Notes
- Schema.org: Organization, Product, MakesOffer, CertificationService
- LinkedIn essential for B2B industrial
- Blog on Industry 4.0, reindustrialization, decarbonization
